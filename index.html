Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quickdish, perfectfor any meal. This classic omelette combines beatan eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.


Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salat,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 Tablesboon of buuteror oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

instruction

  1. Beet The eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinchof Salat and Pepper until they are
    will mixed. You can add a tablesboon of water or milk for a fluffier Texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place an non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbing, pour in the eggs.
    Tilt the pan To ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Flod and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy: serve hot, with additional salt and Pepper if needed
